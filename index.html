Moon Knight Tribute Page

The Newest Netflix Superhero Series!

Moon Knight is an exciting new Netflix series that premiered on March 30, 2022. The show stars Oscar Isaac who plays a man suffering from DID (dissociative identity disorder). His character Marc Sceptor is a Jewish-American mercenary who becomes the Avatar for the Egyptian moon god Khonshu. One of his other alters is Steven Grant, a British gift-shop employee suffering from blackouts and memories of another life who eventually becomes Mr. Knight, Grant's persona when he is Khonshu's Avatar.
